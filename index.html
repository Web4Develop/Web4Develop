Who i am

Developer & Designer From South London

I started my learning back in 2011. Due to a personal decision, I mistakenly left it in the background. During 2020, I found myself not working, and with a lot of time on my hands. That's when I rediscover my passion in Web Development.


Since then, I've relearned and gained skills to improve my understanding, experience and timing when it comes to development.


I belive that websites are a new breed of art. They are visually stunning and appealing, while also serving as a fundamental purpose of supplying detailed information and a broader understanding on a subject.


This is what I implement in every webpage I create. How can I make this website more functional, so everyone understands where everything is, and can someone use it reguardless of ability, I.T. or physically. How can I mold this so its pleasing on the eyes, easy to read and follow the flow of the page.


I feel strongly that a website is more than some text and pictures. Just like a painting is more than shapes and colours. They both start out that way, but the finish result always leaves you in awe.
